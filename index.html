About Me

My name is Jiho John Kim. I am currently an aspiring web developer, even to my suprise. I graduated with a Business Degree from UC Irvine. I had little to no exposure to coding in the past, but after careful consideration, I decided that I really wanted a new challenge and enrolled in this Coding Bootcamp.


Although I am a Korean-American born in Los Angeles, I spent most of my life in Vietnam and Thailand. By living overseas, I got to have a very wide worldview and also learn to interact with and respect many types of cultures. I think this helped me get a very different perspective comapred to someome who lived in just one country their whole life. Seeing the hard lifestyle in countries like Vietnam and Thailand inspired me to try and create things that can help out people in such countries.


Through this Bootcamp, I hope to learn many new skills and continue to challenge myself moving forward.. I believe that working with technology in any shape or form is very important, and coding is one of the bestways to do so. My dream is to be able to create a product that will change many lives.
